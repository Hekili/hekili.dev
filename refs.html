Action List Expressions and Conditions

The syntax for Hekili's priorities are based closely on SimulationCraft. In most cases, an expression will directly match SimulationCraft. Occasionally, the expression is revised or reformatted to function when translated to Lua. This resource is intended to aid in translation between a SimulationCraft priority and profile vs. a Hekili priority pack.
